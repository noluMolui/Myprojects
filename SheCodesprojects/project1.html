Afro Hair

The History


A hairstyle originating with Black people, in which naturally curly or frizzy hair is cut into a full, bushy shape all over the head.the history of afro hair is really interesting. Afro hair has been present in many cultures for centuries, from ancient Egypt to modern-day Africa. In the United States, afro hair was historically seen as a sign of pride and resistance during the Civil Rights Movement. Today, afro hair is often celebrated as a beautiful, unique, and versatile hair type.
